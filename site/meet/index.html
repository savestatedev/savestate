06 / The Edge

Why SaveState

AES-256-GCM Encryption
Your keys, derived from your passphrase via Argon2. We never see them. We never store them. Military-grade encryption, user-controlled.
Incremental Snapshots
Only captures what changed since your last snapshot. Git-style versioning for AI memory — efficient, fast, and space-conscious.
Open Archive Format
The SaveState Archive Format (SAF) is an open specification. Read it, build on it, audit it. Your data in a format you can always parse.
Cross-Platform Migration
Move your AI identity between platforms with a single command. ChatGPT to Claude. Claude to Gemini. No manual re-training.
CLI-First
Built for developers who ship. Scriptable, composable, CI/CD-friendly. Runs everywhere Node.js runs — macOS, Linux, Windows.
