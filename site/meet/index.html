The Problem

Your AI knows everything about you.
What's your backup plan?

🔒

No Portability

Locked into one platform. Switch to a new AI and start from scratch — months of context, gone.

💾

No Backups

No scheduled snapshots, no versioning, no safety net. One API change and your AI's memory vanishes.

🔓

No Control

Your conversations, preferences, and context live on someone else's servers with no encryption you control.

How It Works

Three commands. That's it.

Initialize once, snapshot anytime, restore anywhere.

1

Initialize

Set up encryption and choose your AI platform.

$ savestate init
# Set passphrase, pick adapter
2

Snapshot

Capture everything — conversations, memory, config.

$ savestate snapshot
# Encrypted & versioned
3

Restore

Bring it all back, on any platform.

$ savestate restore latest
# Same or different platform
Why SaveState

Everything you'd expect.
And then some.

🔐

Encrypted at Rest

AES-256-GCM encryption with keys derived from your passphrase. We never see or store your keys.

🔄

Cross-Platform Migration

Move your AI identity between ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and more with a single command.

📦

Open Archive Format

The SaveState Archive Format is an open spec. Your data, in a format you can always parse. Zero lock-in.

⚡

Incremental Snapshots

Only captures what changed since the last snapshot. Git-style versioning for AI memory.

🖥

6 Platform Adapters

ChatGPT, Claude, Claude Code, Gemini, OpenAI Assistants, and custom agents. More shipping constantly.

⏱

Scheduled Backups

Set it and forget it. Automatic snapshots on your schedule, stored locally or in the cloud.
