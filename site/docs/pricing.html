FAQ

Is the CLI really free forever?

Yes. The CLI and the SAF format are open source. You can use local storage with manual snapshots and one adapter at no cost, forever. We believe the backup tool itself should be free — you're paying for convenience (auto-backups, cloud, multi-platform).

Can I use S3/R2 storage on the Free tier?

Yes! You can bring your own S3 or R2 bucket on any tier. The Free tier uses your own credentials. The Pro/Team tiers include managed cloud storage so you don't have to set anything up.

What counts as "1 adapter" on the Free tier?

You can use any one adapter. Most users only need one — whichever platform their AI runs on. If you work across multiple platforms, Pro unlocks all six.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Monthly billing, cancel anytime. Your existing snapshots remain accessible — they're encrypted on your storage and only you have the key.
