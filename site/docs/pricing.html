FAQ

Is the CLI really free forever?

Yes. The SaveState CLI is open source (MIT licensed). Local snapshots, encryption, incremental backups, search, and diff are all free. The code is on GitHub.

What does "1 platform adapter" mean?

On the Free tier, you can use one adapter at a time (e.g., Claude Code only). Pro and Team unlock all 6 built-in adapters: Clawdbot, Claude Code, Claude Web, OpenAI Assistants, ChatGPT, and Gemini.

Can I use S3 / R2 on the Free tier?

Yes. Storage backends (local, S3, R2, B2) are available on all tiers. You bring your own bucket and credentials. The "Cloud storage (10 GB)" on Pro/Team refers to SaveState-managed storage so you don't have to set up your own.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts, no lock-in. Your encrypted snapshots are yours — they work with the free CLI regardless of your subscription status.
